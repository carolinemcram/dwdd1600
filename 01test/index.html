Digital Citizenship

What is Digital Citizenship?

Digital citizenship is the idea of using technology and the internet responsibly, safely, and respectfully. It's about how you behave online and how your actions affect yourself and others.

What is Digital Law?

Digital law refers to the rules and laws that govern how people use technology, the internet, and digital information. It explains what is legal and illegal online.

What is AUP?

It is a set of rules that explains how technology and internet resources are allowed to be used in a school, workplace, or organization.
